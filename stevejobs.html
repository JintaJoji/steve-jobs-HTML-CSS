Biography

Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology enterpreneur and inventor.He was the cofounder,chairman,and CEO of Apple Inc,CEO and largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studio;and founder,chairman,and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s,along with Apple co-founder steve Wozinak.
